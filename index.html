About Our Cause

Here at Back the Block, we're tackling the issue of gentrification in communities local to the Charlotte/Mecklenburg area. Driven to stop the disproportionate rates of displacement that affect our black and brown communities, we've teamed up with local initiatives and organizations to apply pressure directly to our local legislature to ensure all people have access to affordable, secure housing.

Local Legislation

Charlotte/Mecklenburg has a long history of gentrification and displacement. Through beautification efforts, renovation, and construction, our black and brown communities are left displaced, leading to increased homelessness and food insecurity rates. We're here to put a stop to aggressove and neglectful displacement through legislation. In support of The Charlotte Future 2040 Comprehensive Plan, we will ensure that progress can be made while protecting the security and quality of life of our local and long-time Charlotte residents.

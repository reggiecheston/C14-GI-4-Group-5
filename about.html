Who Are We?

Here at Back The Block we believe that every community, regardless of its economic or social status, deserves the opportunity to thrive while preserving its cultural heritage. Our dedicated team of advocates work hard to raise awareness about the impact of gentrification on vulnerable neighborhoods and empower local residents to actively participate in shaping their own destiny.

Gentrification in Charlotte

Gentrification in the Charlotte, NC area may initially aim to stimulate economic development by attracting businesses and opportunities. However, it often results in the displacement of lower-income residents, erodes the distinctive character and cultural essence of a community, and exacerbates issues of inequality. This phenomenon is not just a distant concept; it's visible right in our own neighborhoods, such as NoDa. As new businesses, restaurants, and high-end housing developments flourish in NoDa, property values soar, leading to escalating rents and housing prices. This, in turn, poses challenges for long-standing residents and artists who struggle to afford to remain in the neighborhood. Unfortunately, this issue is pervasive in the Charlotte area, and we are committed to raising awareness about it.
